CYBERTECH INTRODUCTION

  • 01

    VISION OF INNOVATION

    Hong Kong Science Park is the epicenter of technological advancement in Asia. Our mission is to accelerate innovation through cutting-edge research, development, and cross-disciplinary collaboration.

    香港科學園是亞洲科技進步的中心。我們的使命是通過尖端研究、開發和跨學科合作來加速創新。

  • 02

    STATE-OF-THE-ART INFRASTRUCTURE

    Featuring quantum computing labs, neural network research centers, advanced prototyping facilities, and collaborative innovation spaces designed for the future of technology.

    配備量子計算實驗室、神經網絡研究中心、先進原型製作設施以及為技術未來設計的協作創新空間。

  • 03

    DIVERSE TECHNOLOGY FOCUS

    Spanning biotechnology, AI & robotics, fintech, smart city technologies, green tech, nanotechnology, and quantum computing.

    涵蓋生物技術、人工智能與機器人技術、金融科技、智慧城市技術、綠色科技、納米技術和量子計算。

  • 04

    GLOBAL COLLABORATION NETWORK

    Connecting with tech hubs in Silicon Valley, Shenzhen, Tokyo, Berlin, and Singapore to facilitate international knowledge exchange and collaborative research.

    與矽谷、深圳、東京、柏林和新加坡的科技中心連接，促進國際知識交流和協作研究。

  • 05

    TALENT DEVELOPMENT

    Cultivating the next generation of innovators through AI bootcamps, quantum computing workshops, startup incubators, and industry mentorship programs.

    通過AI訓練營、量子計算工作坊、初創企業孵化器和行業導師計劃培養下一代創新者。

TECH ECOSYSTEM & FEATURES

AI & ROBOTICS CENTER

人工智能與機器人中心

The AI & Robotics Center at HKSTP provides state-of-the-art facilities for developing intelligent systems, machine learning algorithms, neural networks, and advanced robotic solutions for various industries including healthcare, logistics, and manufacturing.

香港科技園的人工智能與機器人中心提供最先進的設施，用於開發智能系統、機器學習算法、神經網絡以及用於醫療保健、物流和製造等各行業的先進機器人解決方案。

BIOTECH INNOVATION

生物技術創新

Advanced laboratories for pharmaceutical research, genetic engineering, bioinformatics, and medical device development with CRISPR technology integration.

用於藥物研究、基因工程、生物信息學和醫療設備開發的先進實驗室，集成CRISPR技術。

SMART CITY SOLUTIONS

智慧城市解決方案

Developing IoT systems, sustainable infrastructure, data-driven urban management technologies, and autonomous transportation solutions.

開發物聯網系統、可持續基礎設施、數據驅動的城市管理技術和自動交通解決方案。

QUANTUM COMPUTING

量子計算

Hong Kong's first quantum computing lab with research in quantum algorithms, cryptography, and materials science for next-generation computing.

香港首個量子計算實驗室，研究量子算法、密碼學和用於下一代計算的材料科學。

COLLABORATIVE INNOVATION ENVIRONMENT

協作創新環境

The park fosters cross-disciplinary collaboration between startups, corporations, and academic institutions, creating a dynamic ecosystem where ideas can flourish and transform into viable technologies through our innovation pipeline.

科學園促進初創企業、公司和學術機構之間的跨學科合作，創造一個充滿活力的生態系統，使想法能夠通過我們的創新流程蓬勃發展並轉化為可行的技術。
