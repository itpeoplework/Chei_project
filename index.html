Introduction to Technology at Hong Kong Science Park

  • 1

    Vision of Innovation

    Hong Kong Science Park is dedicated to fostering innovation and technological advancement. It serves as a hub for research and development, bringing together brilliant minds and cutting-edge technologies.

    香港科學園致力於促進創新和技術進步。它作為研發中心，匯聚了卓越人才和尖端技術。

  • 2

    State-of-the-Art Infrastructure

    The park offers world-class facilities including advanced laboratories, collaborative workspaces, and prototyping workshops to support technology development from concept to commercialization.

    科學園提供世界一流的設施，包括先進實驗室、協作工作空間和原型製作工場，以支持技術從概念到商業化的發展。

  • 3

    Diverse Technology Focus

    Spanning biotechnology, AI & robotics, fintech, smart city technologies, and green tech, the park cultivates innovation across multiple high-growth sectors.

    涵蓋生物技術、人工智能與機器人技術、金融科技、智慧城市技術和綠色科技，科學園在多個高增長領域培育創新。

  • 4

    Global Collaboration Network

    With partnerships spanning across Asia, Europe, and North America, the park facilitates international knowledge exchange and collaborative research projects.

    通過遍布亞洲、歐洲和北美的合作夥伴關係，科學園促進國際知識交流和協作研究項目。

  • 5

    Talent Development

    The park nurtures the next generation of innovators through incubator programs, mentorship, and connections to venture capital and industry expertise.

    科學園通過孵化計劃、導師指導以及與風險投資和行業專業知識的聯繫，培育下一代創新者。

Technology Ecosystem & Features

AI & Robotics Center

人工智能與機器人中心

The AI & Robotics Center at HKSTP provides state-of-the-art facilities for developing intelligent systems, machine learning algorithms, and advanced robotic solutions for various industries.

香港科技園的人工智能與機器人中心為開發智能系統、機器學習算法和用於各行業的先進機器人解決方案提供最先進的設施。

Biotech Innovation

生物技術創新

Advanced laboratories for pharmaceutical research, genetic engineering, and medical device development.

用於藥物研究、基因工程和醫療設備開發的先進實驗室。

Smart City Solutions

智慧城市解決方案

Developing IoT systems, sustainable infrastructure, and data-driven urban management technologies.

開發物聯網系統、可持續基礎設施和數據驅動的城市管理技術。

Green Technology Hub

綠色科技中心

Focus on renewable energy, waste management innovations, and sustainable materials development.

專注於可再生能源、廢物管理創新和可持續材料開發。

Collaborative Innovation Environment

協作創新環境

The park fosters cross-disciplinary collaboration between startups, corporations, and academic institutions, creating a dynamic ecosystem where ideas can flourish and transform into viable technologies.

科學園促進初創企業、公司和學術機構之間的跨學科合作，創造一個充滿活力的生態系統，使想法得以蓬勃發展並轉化為可行的技術。
